This Privacy Policy explains how EzBrewDay ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Service. We believe in keeping things simple and transparent — we collect only what we need to run the app, and we never sell your data.

01

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate the Service:

  • Email address — collected at account creation, used for authentication, password resets, and service communications
  • Brew data — recipes, brew day notes, settings, and calculations you create within the app, stored in your cloud account
  • Payment information — processed entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your credit card number, expiry, or CVV

We do not collect your name, address, phone number, or any other personal information beyond what is listed above.

02

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and manage your account
  • To store and sync your brew data across sessions and devices
  • To process payments and manage your Pro subscription status
  • To send transactional emails (password resets, purchase confirmations)
  • To respond to support requests sent to support@ezbrewday.com

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating and improving the Service.

03

04

What We Don't Do

We will never:
Sell your personal data · Share your email with advertisers · Use your brew data for any purpose other than running your account · Display ads in the app

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a valid court order, subpoena, or legal process. We will notify you of such requests where legally permitted to do so.

05

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account remains active. If you cancel your subscription, your brew data remains accessible as a free account unless you request deletion.

To delete your account and all associated data, email support@ezbrewday.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request." We will process your request within 30 days and confirm once complete.

06

Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data:

  • All connections are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Authentication and password storage are handled by Supabase — passwords are never stored in plain text
  • Payment data is handled exclusively by Stripe's PCI-compliant infrastructure

While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your EzBrewDay account.

07

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access the data we hold about you
Correct inaccurate data
Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
Object to or restrict processing
Export your brew data (portability)
Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@ezbrewday.com. We will respond within 30 days.

08

Children's Privacy

EzBrewDay is a homebrewing application and is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@ezbrewday.com and we will delete it promptly.

09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or by email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Your continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10

Contact & Data Requests

For privacy questions, data access requests, or deletion requests:

Email: support@ezbrewday.com
Subject line for data requests: "Privacy Request" or "Account Deletion Request"
Response time: Within 30 days
